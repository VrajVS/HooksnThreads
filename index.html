How it works

Three soft steps
to your piece.

Every order is hooked by hand, in the colours you pick. Lead time is usually 7-14 days depending on the size of the piece.

  1. 01

    Choose

    Browse the catalogue and note the items you love. Pick your colours — yarn shades can be matched on request.

  2. 02

    Message

    Send a DM on Instagram @hooksnthreads_official or WhatsApp with the product, quantity and colours.

  3. 03

    Receive

    We confirm timing & cost, you pay, and your handmade piece reaches you wrapped and ready in 1–2 weeks.
